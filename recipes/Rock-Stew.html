Rock Stew. Yummy!

This recipe is BIG and BOLD. Take a good look at this planet sized picture

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rocks

Directions

  1. Boil a large pot of water on the stove-top
  2. Add as many rocks as desired
  3. Stir for 53 hours. Do not stop stirring or the rocks may burn
  4. Serve fresh for best flavour
