Office Beans

These aren't your normal beans. Sure to make a splash at your next office party.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Salsa
  • Spices of choice

Directions

  1. Mix 10 cans of beans with 5 jars of salsa
  2. Stir in spices of your choice to taste
  3. Heat in a stainless steel pot on the stove-top at high for 45 minutes
